About

Fresh catch Co., Ltd Established in 2019, registers in Tiko Municipality, we are specialized in;cat fish hatching ang growing, tylapi fertilisation/hatching and growing short cycled crops ( cucumber, watermelon peas and green beans) hence we are integrating development and production together. Fresh catch co has in plan to export its produce to south-east Asia, USA, EU, etc, and has established business relationships with some international organizations(names hidded for administrative reasons) Fresh catch co has as plan to be the biggest supplier of government projects in Cameroon, Ethopia, central Africa republic, Gabon, South Africa, Ecuador, Indonesia, etc. Our water tanks solve local drought problems; fish tanks provide the indoor and outdoor farming system; helping energy conservation, reducing carbon emission, and improving rural living conditions as per environmental/health checks. Our greatest asset is our human resource, a vibrant, skilled and cohesive team which is giving us a competitive edge.
